About X Premium Organizations

Premium Organizations is our premium subscription offering for governments and multilateral organizations.

Are you a business? Learn more about X Premium Business.

 

We’re creating the most trusted place on the internet for government entities to verify their affiliations and engage with citizens.

X Premium Organizations enables government entities of all sizes – from local municipalities to national agencies and multilateral organizations – to sign up and manage their verification and to affiliate and verify any related account. Please see Purchaser Terms of Service for more info.

Any government entity or multilateral organization that purchases a subscription to Premium Organizations will receive a grey checkmark and square avatar.

In addition, accounts subscribed to Premium Organizations can choose to affiliate any individual or entity associated with them. An affiliated account receives verification (denoted by either a blue or grey checkmark) as well as an affiliate badge, a small image of their parent organization's profile picture, displayed next to their checkmark. Clicking or pressing this badge takes a user directly to the affiliated organization, making it a great driver of engagement from your whole network on X.

For example, a government can affiliate its departments, officials, or other entities. There is no limit to the number of affiliates an organization can have, or who they can affiliate (as long as they are related). All vetted and approved government organizations and government individuals accounts can receive a grey checkmark and affiliate badge if they sign up for Premium Organizations.

By subscribing, government entities can access our new tool to manage verification, meaning you have full control over your entity's presence on X. You’ll be able to add and remove affiliated accounts at any time. Affiliates must accept an invite in order to receive verification.

An X Premium Organizations Full Access monthly tier subscription is $1,000/month (plus any applicable tax and fees) and $50/month (plus any applicable tax and fees) for each additional affiliate in the U.S. Full Access annual tier subscription is $10,000/year (plus any applicable tax and fees) and $600/year (plus any applicable tax and fees) for each additional affiliate in the U.S. Prices may vary by region and are subject to change. Click here for pricing information.

Features

Equip your government entity with tools to enhance public communication, recruit skilled personnel, build credibility, and access exclusive public insights.

Premium Organizations elevates and distinguishes government entities and their affiliates on X. Grey checkmarks, square avatars, and custom affiliation badges verify and differentiate your entity's main account, officials, and subsidiary accounts. We will continue to improve our service and may change our features from time to time.

X reserves the right without notice to remove your checkmark or other badging at any time at its sole discretion, including if you violate our Purchaser Terms, Terms of Service, and policies, or if your account is suspended or violates any of our rules or policies.

 

Premium Organizations Portal

We’ve launched an entirely new feature on X that lets any Premium Organizations organization sign up, manage their billing, and verify their network.

The Premium Organizations portal lets organizations add and remove affiliated accounts, and provides immediate verification status to anyone in their network who accepts their invitation to affiliate. This could be for new officials, a newly established agency, or changes in government teams.

 

Affiliations

The most reliable information comes directly from official sources on X. Now, you can affiliate your entity’s representatives so that everyone knows their official affiliation. Affiliates receive a small image of their organization's account profile picture next to their name every time they post, send a DM, or appear in search.

You can affiliate anyone who represents or is associated with your government entity: leadership, officials, departments, spokespeople, agencies, and so on. An account you invite to affiliate must accept your invitation before they receive the affiliation badge.

 

Custom Organization Profile

Premium Organizations accounts receive a number of customizations to their profile. In addition to grey checkmarks, government entities also receive a square avatar, clearly distinguishing them from other accounts.

All Premium Organizations accounts also receive a new tab on their profile that lists all affiliated accounts. This appears next to the posts tab and displays any affiliated accounts.

With affiliation badges and the affiliation tab, this makes it easy for visitors to click on the badge of an affiliate, arrive at the organization’s profile, and see all the other affiliated accounts in one place.

 

Impersonation Defense

All Premium Organizations and affiliated accounts are defended against impersonation. Accounts are monitored for changes (including display names, profile photos, and usernames) and flagged for further review if impersonation is detected.

In addition, all Premium Organizations entities have access to a VIP Support team, which enables you to quickly escalate any critical issues.

 

Priority Support

Premium Organizations accounts have access to a VIP Support team directly from their Premium Organizations portals.

Government entities experiencing issues, including with onboarding, removing or adding affiliates, billing, or issues with impersonation of your accounts, can contact VIP Support and we will ensure timely support and escalations.

Premium Organizations VIP Support is reserved for the Premium Organizations itself and its affiliates only. Submitting requests on behalf of other accounts is considered misuse of the feature and may result in loss of access.

 

Elevated Post, DM, and Media Upload Limits

Premium Organizations accounts and their affiliates have significantly elevated post, DM, and media upload rate limits. Premium Organizations subscribers can use this to provide seamless public updates, live-post events, or run informational campaigns.

 

Hiring

Seamlessly integrate positions from your careers site and make them discoverable to millions of relevant candidates. Premium Organizations accounts can feature key openings directly on their profile, driving traffic to position listings.

Additionally, Premium Organizations accounts unlock advanced job advertising capabilities, allowing you to target relevant users directly in their timelines.

 

Radar (Beta)

Introducing Radar. Monitor keywords, track conversations, and stay on top of public trends you care about.

Radar is available to Premium Organizations accounts with full functionality. Functionality may vary by subscription.

 

X Premium+

All accounts (the main organization and their affiliates) subscribed to Premium Organizations will receive all the benefits of X Premium+. You’ll get access to features like access to SuperGrok (xAI’s Premium Grok subscription), Radar, Edit post, Bookmark Folders, Longer posts, Prioritized rankings in conversations, and Reduced ads.*

See more on X Premium+ here.

*Not all features available on all platforms and locations. X Premium+ and Premium Organizations features may change periodically as we keep improving the service.

How to apply

Sign up today

Follow these steps to onboard to Premium Organizations:

  1. Sign up and add your eligibility criteria to the form
  2. Add your payment details
  3. Your account will be reviewed
  4. Initially you will be able to leverage all the benefits of X Premium+, but your government account will not receive a grey checkmark and you will not be able to add affiliated accounts until your account is approved.
  5. Once reviewed and approved, your account will immediately be verified and you will be able to add accounts as affiliates.

Please note: some restrictions apply. Also review our Purchaser Terms of Service.
 

Criteria for eligibility

  •  A government entity must provide a matching active X account, current official email address, and current website domain when they sign up.

  • A government entity must have access to and manage the matching active account they wish to verify. They will be required to authenticate their account when they activate their new subscription.

  • Government entities must provide an official government email address (e.g., “.gov”).

  • A government entity's profile must be complete with an accurate profile photo, bio, and display name.

  • Only government entities (such as official agencies, institutions, multilateral organizations, and key officials like heads of state, cabinet members, spokespeople, ambassadors) are allowed on Premium Organizations. If you are a business or other entity, please apply to Premium Business.

  • Personal or individual accounts cannot subscribe to Premium Organizations. Some exceptions can be made for certain government-related accounts, which our team assesses on a case-by-case basis.

We support credit card payments and invoicing. Accounts which sign up for Premium Organizations but are not a government or multilateral entity will not be refunded.

Availability

The Premium Organizations Portal is a web-only, enterprise feature, meaning management and billing via the portal are exclusively available on web.

Accounts who receive checkmarks, affiliation badges, and Premium+ can manage their settings on the web, and in the iOS and Android apps.

Premium Organizations is available globally. See our pricing here.

 

How to use Premium Organizations

The X Premium Organizations portal is designed to be as flexible and easy to use as possible. Once your organization is onboarded, you will be able to add and remove affiliated accounts, and manage your advertising credit and billing in one place.

How to activate your account

  1. Any government entity or multilateral organization can sign up directly in the app via this link.

  2. You will be prompted to choose a payment method: Direct or Invoicing: a. Direct payments support credit cards and some forms of wire transfer and let you pay upfront and get access immediately. b. Invoicing is for purchase orders above 5 affiliates, allowing organizations to bulk order and process internally. Purchases can be made via wire and bank transfer and must be completed within 60 days.

  3. Once you complete your purchase your account will be reviewed per our criteria above and approved if it meets them.

  4. You’ll be notified (via email) that your account is ready to activate. You can also follow this link to complete onboarding.

  5. Your account will be ready to use and you can begin adding affiliated accounts.

How to add affiliated accounts

Premium Organizations lets any organization add accounts affiliated with their organization. You are responsible for any account you affiliate as per our Purchaser Terms of Service, Terms of Service, and other policies. To add affiliated accounts:

  1. Click Add accounts.

  2. A popup will appear where you can search for any account.

  3. You can add as many affiliates as you want. These accounts must be related to your entity and conform to our Purchaser Terms of Service.

  4. To add an affiliate click the “Send Invite” button.

  5. When you send an invite, the affiliated user will be notified (via email and push notification) that you have invited them to join.

  6. They will be added to the “Pending” tab in the Portal.

  7. Once the user accepts, they will immediately get a checkmark and affiliate badge, and they will move from the “Pending” tab to the “Accounts” tab.

  8. You can change an affiliate's verification type by switching between individual (blue checkmark) and organization (grey checkmark) in the portal. Accounts for individuals must be labeled as individuals (blue checkmark), and organization accounts must be labeled as organizations (grey checkmark).

How to remove affiliated accounts

  1. You can remove affiliated accounts quickly and easily by clicking the “X” remove button in the row next to any affiliated account.

  2. You’ll be prompted to confirm with “Are you sure you want to remove @handle?”

  3. Clicking “Yes, do it” will immediately remove both verification and affiliation badges for the affiliated account.

  4. Note: The removed affiliated account will lose verification and affiliation but you will continue to be billed for the affiliated account until the end of the next billing cycle for that account.

How to use your advertising credit

Premium Organizations receive an ads credit to spend on advertising on X every month for a limited time. The credit can be used on either your organization account or any of your affiliates. See our terms here See our terms here. 

In order to use your Premium Organizations ad credit, you must take the following steps:

 

  1. Find your Ads Account ID associated with your X account. You can find this here. 

  2. Make sure you add a credit card as a payment type in the Ads Manager by selecting “Add new payment method.” If you don’t have a credit card set up your credit will not be applied and your ad will not run.

  3. Copy your Ads Account ID into the text input within the Premium Organizations portal here. Click Activate.

  4. Launch your campaign. 

 

If you are a new advertiser 

  • Find your Ads Account ID associated with your X account. You can find this here. 

  • Make sure you add a credit card as a payment type in the Ads Manager by selecting “Add new payment method.” If you don’t have a credit card set up your credit will not be applied and your ad will not run.

  • Copy the Ads Account ID into the text input within the Premium Organizations portal here. Click Activate.

  • You will see your credit applied under Billing in the Ads Manager.

 

If you have advertised before

  • If you have advertised with X in the past, you may have multiple Ads Account IDs.

  • You can find a list of all your Ads Account IDs here.

  • Make sure you choose the correct Ads Account ID to apply your credit. 

  • Copy the Ads Account ID into the text input within the Premium Organizations portal here. Click activate.

  • You will see your credit applied under Billing in the Ads Manager.

 

If you would like to use your credit on an affiliated account 

  •  

    As a Premium Organizations subscriber you can apply your credit on any of your affiliated accounts.

     

  • To do this, you must have access to the Ads Account ID of the affiliated account you’d like to run a campaign on.

  • You can find this by logging into the affiliated account and navigating here. 

  • Copy the Ads Account ID into the text input within the Premium Organizations portal here. Click Activate.

  • You will see your credit applied under Billing in the Ads Manager.

Ads credits are issued at the start of each billing cycle and are subject to activation and usage timelines:

Activation window:

  •  

  • Monthly subscriptions: Credits must be activated and added to a valid X Ads account within 60 days of issuance.

  • Yearly subscriptions: Credits must be activated and added to a valid X ads account within 120 days of issuance.

Usage period:

  • Monthly subscriptions: Activated credits must be used within 365 days.

  • Yearly subscriptions: Activated credits must be used within 730 days.

Credits are contingent upon maintaining an active Premium Organizations subscription. If your subscription is canceled or terminated, any unused or active credits will be invalidated and cannot be redeemed. Similarly, if you change your subscription, any unused or active credits will be invalidated and cannot be redeemed. See our terms here. 

How to manage your jobs
  1. Navigate to the Hiring tab in your Portal.
  2. Add your positions through a supported Applicant Tracking System (ATS) integration or manually using the 'Add job' button. If you would like to integrate using an XML feed, please contact our support team.
  3. Feature your top roles on your profile by clicking 'Start showing my jobs'. You can select which specific roles are featured by toggling on 'Featured' for individual jobs.
  4. To share your jobs, click the 3-dot menu next to the job and select 'Copy link to job' to share across your timeline, communities, and DMs.
  5. To promote your jobs, click the Promote button to launch a new advertising campaign.
How to cancel your subscription

  1. To cancel your subscription, navigate to the Settings tab in your Portal.

  2. Click the cancellation link. 

  3. Canceling Premium Organizations will result in your organization immediately losing its grey checkmark. All affiliated accounts will be immediately removed from your organization’s profile and lose their grey or blue checkmarks and affiliate badges.

  4. You will not be refunded for the remaining period left on your subscription before your next billing cycle. You will not be billed after that.

How to manage your billing

  1. Navigate to the Billing tab in your Portal.

  2. You’ll be taken to our payment provider’s, Stripe, customer billing portal.

  3. Here, you’ll be able to manage your billing details.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Can the training be customized according to our organization's needs?

      We specialize in delivering live, tailored training aligned to your unique needs. Recognizing that each organization has a unique set of challenges, our team offers flexibility and experience with practical, impactful programs that fit your specific requirements, budget and time frame.

      Do you offer ongoing support or follow-up sessions after the training?

      We focus on building a solid foundation for future growth, involving stakeholders and nurturing enduring change. Depending on your requirements, we are fully equipped to provide requisite ongoing support and follow-ups to solidify the effectiveness of the training long after it's over.

      How is the training structured, and what are the main topics?

      Our training sessions follow a live, presenter-guided format with role-playing exercises that address real-world scenarios. Topics typically include conflict resolution, negotiation, trust-building, optimization of meetings, and fostering inclusive and supportive work environments. The structure, however, is adaptable to your organization's unique context and the specific challenges you wish to address.

      How long does the training last?

      Our training offers exceptional flexibility in terms of duration. You can choose from a concise, intense 60-minute presentation and a detailed and exhaustive one-week workshop tailored to meet your specific needs. The goal is to provide you with the knowledge and tools you need within the time span that works best for your organization.

      How much does the training cost?

      Because each organization has unique needs, our pricing model is flexible. The cost of the training is personalized according to your project requirements and budget. We strive to offer quality content delivery at a price that aligns with your budget so that your organization receives the most value from our program.

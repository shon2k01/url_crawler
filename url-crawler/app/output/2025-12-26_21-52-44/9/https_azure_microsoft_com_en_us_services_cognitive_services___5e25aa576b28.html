    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions

    • Foundry Tools are Microsoft’s suite of prebuilt AI capabilities—Vision, Speech, Language, Translator, Content Understanding, and Document Intelligence—designed to help organizations quickly add advanced AI features to their apps and agents. These tools are fully managed, scalable, and part of the unified Microsoft Foundry platform, making it easier to innovate and transform business processes.
    • Foundry Tools is the new name for the product collection previously known as Azure AI Services or Azure Cognitive Services.

      Foundry Tools are part of the broader Foundry platform, which unifies models, agents, and tools under one developer-friendly experience.
    • Each service has pricing that is customizable to the application or use case.
      Learn more
    • Foundry Tools have a lower barrier to entry and are designed for all types of users, while Azure machine learning is primarily aimed toward data scientists who are looking to design a more bespoke solution.
      Learn more

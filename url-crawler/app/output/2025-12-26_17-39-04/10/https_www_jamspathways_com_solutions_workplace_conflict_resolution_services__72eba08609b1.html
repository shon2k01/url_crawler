      Frequently Asked Questions

      What is JAMS Pathways?

      JAMS Pathways offers proactive conflict resolution and facilitation services, focusing on early intervention, dialogue, and leadership training to prevent disputes and promote a positive workplace culture.

      How does JAMS Pathways differ from traditional dispute resolution services?

      Unlike traditional alternative dispute resolution (ADR) services that address conflicts after they arise, JAMS Pathways emphasizes early intervention, aiming to prevent disputes and foster a collaborative work environment.

      What types of organizations can benefit from JAMS Pathways services?

      JAMS Pathways provides customized workplace conflict resolution, training, and facilitation services suitable for corporations, nonprofits, government agencies, and educational institutions facing workplace conflicts or leadership challenges.

      What is the PATH program?

      The PATH program is a structured, four-phase approach to conflict resolution:
      1. Preliminary Assessment â€“ Identifying challenges and needs through stakeholder interviews and surveys.
      2. Analysis & Early Intervention â€“ Designing tailored interventions based on gathered insights.
      3. Targeted Workshops & Conversations â€“ Facilitating workshops and dialogues to uncover issues and develop solutions.
      4. Highlighting Progress & Mapping Next Steps â€“ Reflecting on accomplishments and planning for sustained success.

      What training programs are offered?

      JAMS Pathways offers various training programs, including: 
      - Conflict Resolution 101 
      - Navigating Workplace Tension 
      - Negotiation Skills 101 
      These programs are designed to equip teams with essential skills to manage and resolve conflicts effectively.

      How are the programs delivered?

      Programs can be delivered in-person, virtually, or in a hybrid format, depending on the organization's needs and preferences.

      What is the duration of the training programs?

      Training durations are flexible, ranging from 60-minute sessions to one-week workshops, tailored to meet the specific needs of the organization.

      How is pricing determined?

      Pricing is customized based on the specific needs and scope of each training, aiming to provide the best value considering the duration and complexity required.

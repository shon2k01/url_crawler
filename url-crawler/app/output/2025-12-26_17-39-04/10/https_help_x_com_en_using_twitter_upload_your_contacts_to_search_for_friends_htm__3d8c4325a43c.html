How to upload and manage your contacts

When you upload your contacts to your account, we can help you find people you may know on X. Then you’ll be able to find and follow contacts who have allowed other people to find them by their email address or phone number. We may also use your imported address book contacts to personalize content, such as making suggestions or showing user accounts and posts for you and other people. Learn more about how we use your uploaded contacts to make account suggestions and about other ways of finding people to follow on X.

You can control whether X will use your address book to suggest your account to others by adjusting the privacy settings that let others find you by your email address or phone number. 

 

To find contacts on X
Step 1

In the top menu, tap your profile icon, then tap Settings and privacy.

Step 2

Tap Privacy and safety. 

Step 3

Under Discoverability and contacts, tap Discoverability and contacts.

Step 4

Tap Sync address book contacts. When you sync your contacts, contacts from your device’s address book will be uploaded to X on an ongoing basis.

Step 5

The accounts of contacts from your address book who are already on X will be shown.

To remove your contacts, follow the steps below. 
To stop syncing address book contacts on X for iOS or Android app

You can stop uploading your contacts to X on an ongoing basis by adjusting the Sync address book contacts setting.

Using X for iOS:

  1. In the top menu, tap your profile icon.
  2. Tap Settings and privacy, then tap Privacy and safety.
  3. Under Discoverability and contacts, tap Discoverability and contacts.
  4. Next to Sync address book contacts, drag the slider to turn off.

 

Using X for Android:

  1. In the top menu, you will either see a navigation menu icon  or your profile icon. Tap whichever icon you have.
  2. Tap Settings and privacy, then tap Privacy and safety.
  3. Under Discoverability and contacts, tap Discoverability and contacts.
  4. Next to Sync address book contacts, uncheck the box to turn off.

Note: When you turn off your Sync address book contacts setting on a given device, this will stop syncing contacts to X from that device only. If you want to stop syncing your contacts from other devices, you will need to adjust your settings on those devices or remove all contacts from X. X will continue to store and use the contacts you’ve previously uploaded unless you remove all contacts.


To remove contacts on your X for iOS or Android app
 

  1. In the top menu, tap your profile icon (iOS) or a navigation menu icon  or your profile icon (Android).
  2. Tap Settings and privacy, then tap Privacy and safety.
  3. Under Discoverability and contacts, tap Discoverability and contacts.
  4. Tap Remove all contacts. You’ll need to confirm the removal of all contacts by tapping the prompt.
     

Note: This will remove any contacts you’ve previously uploaded and turn off your account’s Sync address book contacts setting on any devices from which you previously chose to sync contacts. Please be aware that this takes a little time and you may still notice some suggestions on X (based on your contacts) in the meantime.
 

How to view and remove contacts via X.com
To view or remove contacts:

You can view or remove previously imported contacts from X at any time. Please note your account recommendations may not be as relevant after removing this information.

  1. Click on the more  menu.
  2. Go to Settings and privacy.
  3. Click Privacy and safety.
  4. Click Discoverability and contacts.
  5. Click Manage contacts.
  6. You may be prompted to enter your password. All of your uploaded contacts will be displayed.
  7. If you’d like to remove your contacts, click Remove all contacts. Once you confirm this request by clicking Remove, any contacts you’ve previously uploaded will be removed from X and your account’s Sync Address Book Contacts setting will be turned off on any devices from which you previously chose to sync contacts. Please be aware that this takes a little time and you may still notice some suggestions on X (based on your contacts) in the meantime.
     

To remove contacts via mobile.X.com:

  1. Tap your profile icon.
  2. Tap Settings and privacy, then tap Privacy and safety.
  3. Under Discoverability and contacts, tap Discoverability and contacts.
  4. Tap Remove contacts. You’ll need to confirm the removal of all contacts by tapping the prompt.

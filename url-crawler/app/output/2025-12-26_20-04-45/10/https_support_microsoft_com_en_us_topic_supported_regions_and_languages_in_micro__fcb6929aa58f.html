    Supported regions and languages in Microsoft Copilot

    Note: Information on this page applies to Microsoft Copilot. If you're using Microsoft 365 Copilot for work, please see Supported languages for Microsoft 365 Copilot.

    Microsoft Copilot is available globally in over 170 markets around the world. There are a few exceptions for regions like China (excluding Hong Kong) and embargoed markets where Copilot is either not available or not supported. 

    In some cases when a new feature is released in Copilot, it may not be immediately available in all regions or languages.

What is Copilot Search?

Copilot Search in Bing gives you quick, summarized answers with cited sources and suggestions for further exploration, making it easier than ever to discover more. Simply click the Copilot icon Search to get started.

Understanding the Difference:

Search and Copilot Search

Explore how each experience is designed to help you find information, get things done, and go beyond traditional search.

Bing icon Traditional Search

Bing helps you search the web for facts, directions, shopping, and more. With Bing, you can search effortlessly across multiple locations for a smoother experience.

Best for:

  • Finding websites, articles, quick facts, or product info.

Copilot Search icon Copilot Search

Copilot Search takes your questions to the next level, offering contextual answers, helpful summaries, and deeper insights. Its perfect for when you want more than just a list of links.

Best for:

  • Richer context, further exploration and deeper understanding.
